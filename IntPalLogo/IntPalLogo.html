International Pal (Int.Pal)
Imagined Company Logo
Type
Graphic Design Project
When
April 2022 - May 2022
Role
Created a company concept and aesthetic, hand-sketched logo design, and finalized in Adobe Illustrator
Tools
Paper and Pencil, Figma, Adobe Illustrator

International Pal or Int. Pal is where Duolingo meets International Yums. The company embraces having an immersive experience that includes learning the language, eating the cuisine and overall learning about the culture from the country of your choice from people who are from that country; Which would be great if you wanted to meet someone from there. Int.Pal is a subscription service where you engage with others from the country you are interested in and learn about the culture and learn some dishes (ingredients shipped to you) and you can cook together.
